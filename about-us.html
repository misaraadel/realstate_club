About Us

Saudi Real Estate Club.

The Saudi Real Estate Club was founded by Shorooq Alsulaiman in 2014 at Dar Al-Hekma University during her master’s journey, as an initiative created to bring together individuals passionate about the real estate sector, professional development, and business networking.

Over the years, the club has evolved into a professional real estate platform that organizes regular meetings, real estate seminars, and discussion panels, while hosting industry experts and speakers to discuss the future of the real estate sector, investment opportunities, urban development, marketing, and real estate valuation within the Saudi and global markets.

The club aims to build a refined real estate community that brings together ambition, knowledge, and professional relationships under one platform.

Our Vision
