Shrouq Al-Sulaiman

Saudi entrepreneur and real estate investor

A Saudi entrepreneur and real estate investor, and founder of the Saudi Real Estate Club, she possesses extensive experience in real estate development, appraisal, investment, luxury properties, and business leadership.

Her passion and career in the real estate sector began in 2006 after completing her university studies. She worked with local and international real estate companies, which contributed to building in-depth expertise in the areas of development, investment, marketing, and real estate appraisal.

Career and achievements

Specializing in establishing and leading real estate companies towards growth and success.

She contributed to the establishment and management of several real estate and property appraisal companies within the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

I participated in the development of more than 40 real estate units and projects.

They were among the first to adopt the concept of smart residential complexes in the Saudi real estate market.

She was elected Vice President of the Real Estate Committee at the Jeddah Chamber of Commerce in 2010.

A member of the Saudi Entrepreneurs Committee, as one of the first women to establish real estate development companies.

Member of the Saudi National Committee for Real Estate Valuation From 2025

A supporter of women's development and empowerment in the real estate sector since 2011.

She contributed to the development of systems related to women's real estate transactions in Saudi courts and notary offices through developmental initiatives and projects.

A writer for several newspapers and specialized real estate articles.

She is considered one of the most influential figures in the field of real estate social media and awareness, particularly regarding investment and real estate in the Kingdom.

In 2025, I received an award for third place in the real estate influence category on social media.

As an organizer and influencer, I participated in the sale of the highest price per square meter of land in the world in 2025 in Makkah Al-Mukarramah, Ajyad area.
