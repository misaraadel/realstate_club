About Us

A legacy to be built... and a future to be created.

The Real Estate Club was founded in 2014, and in 2020 we embarked on a new era with an ambitious vision to transform the concept of housing in the Kingdom. We believe that the relationship between a person and their home transcends walls; therefore, we have dedicated our expertise to delivering real estate products that combine “quality” and “comfort.” Today, with a portfolio exceeding 6.5 billion riyals and 11 international awards, we continue our journey to be the fastest-growing developer, setting global standards with a local spirit.

Vision

To be the fastest-growing urban developer in the Kingdom by Creating communities that thrive on comfort.

Mission

Empowering every family and business to find the space that suits their needs and fulfills their aspirations, with quality standards that ensure lasting comfort.
